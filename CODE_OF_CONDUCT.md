# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Orbit welcoming and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Protect user privacy and never share private data in project spaces.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when needed, and repair harm.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate exposure of private information, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the repository's security contact channel. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

This code is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
