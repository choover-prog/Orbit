# Responsive Behavior

## Desktop

The attention state places Maya and the Presence to the left of one focal concern, connected by one restrained relationship path. Conversation and action use a centered reading column. Administrative routes use labeled rows rather than dashboard cards.

## Tablet

The focal composition keeps the two-part relationship while reducing gaps and type. Presence Lab controls move to two columns, and context previews stack when comparison would become cramped.

## Phone

- Person and Presence move above the focal concern.
- The relationship becomes a short vertical connector.
- Supporting facts stack instead of compressing.
- Evidence and action details become one-column disclosures.
- The Ask Orbit input remains reachable near the bottom safe area.
- Primary navigation removes low-priority links rather than squeezing a desktop bar.
- The Presence comparison becomes one variant per row.

Playwright validates an iPhone-sized viewport for horizontal overflow and reachability of the concern and text input.
